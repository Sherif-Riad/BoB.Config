Creature Soft Limit
% server population This sets a limit for the total number of a certain creature compared to the rest of the server.
I.E.:50% on pteranodon means that a max of 50% of the server population can be Pteranodons. anyone that exceeds this will have their growth rate SEVERELY reduced.
Creature Growth Limit This sets a limit for the Max growth a creature can attain.
Hard limited to 20
Pack Soft Cap Start This is the growth at which new pack members will start causing the overpacked debuff
Pack Soft Cap End This is the growth at which new pack members will reach max strength of the overpacked debuff
